Our Services & Work Standards

Discover the depth of our craftsmanship, the structural standards we maintain, and the seamless process we bring to every home improvement project.

What We Offer

Roofing & Custom Ceiling Installations

A secure roof is essential to guard your family against Cape Town's intense seasonal weather. We don't just patch up leaks; we provide complete diagnostic checks, timber support frame adjustments, and full roof sheet replacements.

Specialties include:

  • Rhino Board ceiling installations and skimming for a perfectly flat finish
  • Decorative cornice fitting and seamless jointing
  • Full roof waterproofing, ridge capping, and damp prevention

Premium Interior & Exterior Painting

A professional paint job requires expert preparation before a brush ever touches the wall. We meticulously scrape off peeling old coats, fill structural deep wall cracks, and sand surfaces to a completely flat state to guarantee maximum coat adherence.

Specialties include:

  • Weatherproof exterior facade painting built to withstand sun and rain
  • Clean interior accent walls, door frames, and high-durability kitchen finishes
  • Precision masking, zero drips, and total protection of your household furniture

Structural Cement & Masonry Work

Foundations shape the long-term reliability of any expansion or upgrade. We combine accurate aggregate mixing with leveling techniques to deliver crack-free surfaces ready for heavy foot traffic or structural brickwork expansion.

Specialties include:

  • High-strength concrete floor screeding for a flat, glass-smooth base
  • Plastering and leveling raw interior or exterior brick boundary walls
  • Driveways, secure foundations, structural wall patches, and general masonry

Property & Lawn Maintenance

We keep your outdoor space looking pristine and well-managed year-round.

Specialties include:

  • Professional lawn mowing and edging
  • Garden clearing and debris removal
  • General yard upkeep and maintenance

Custom Tiling & Bathroom Renovations

Turn your bathroom into a luxury space. We specialize in precision large-format wall and floor tiling, structural plumbing alignments, and modern fixture mountings with complete damp-proofing seals.

Specialties include:

  • Flawless ceramic and porcelain wall-to-floor tile layouts
  • Modern shower panel, mixer, and dual-head system installations
  • Water-sealed environments to ensure zero moisture seepage or damp buildup
